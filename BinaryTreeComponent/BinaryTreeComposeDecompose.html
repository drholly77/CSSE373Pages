BinaryTree Component - Compose & Decompose

The purpose of the BinaryTree component:

  • To hide the nodes and pointers from the client programmer
  • To provide operations which automatically handle connecting and unconnecting tree nodes
  • To make client programming with binary trees easier

Examples:

#include "BinaryTree/BinaryTree1.hpp"
typedef BinaryTree1<Integer> IntegerTree;
IntegerTree t;
