Setup CppDevSp19 Folder

Overview
  • The CppDevSp19 folder contains software components that you will be using this quarter when writing your software and it is where you will store all your CSSE373 software development lab assignments
  • It is recommended that you place this folder on your computer's C:\ drive, and work exclusively from this folder throughout quarter
Instructions
  1. Download the .zip (below)
  2. Once it is downloaded, navigate into the .zip by double clicking on it
  3. Once inside the zip, copy the folder found inside (called "CppDevSp19") to your C:\ drive
  4. After it is placed onto your C:\ drive, it should look similar to the screen shot to the right
