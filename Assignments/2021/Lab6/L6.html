2. L6 Statement of Work

2.1 Primary Objectives

The primary objective of this lab is to provide you experience with:

  • Layering an additional operation (e.g., Queue append) on an already existing component (e.g., Queue)
  • Learning more about C++'s template and inheritance capabilities

Important Restrictions:

  1. Use Design by Contract - No defensive programming of the flip operation
