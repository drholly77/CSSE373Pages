Create a Moodle Blog Entry

Edit your Moodle profile's biography section by performing the following edits:

Part #1 - Upload a profile picture (or picture/icon that represents you) to your Moodle profile

If prior to this assignment you have already uploaded a picture, then you are done with Part #1

  1. Log into Moodle
  2. Click on Dashboard
  3. Click on the circle where you picture should go
  4. Scroll down to "User details" and click "Edit profile"
  5. Upload your picture

Part #2 - Create a blog entry on Moodle and provide answers to the following questions

Follow the steps listed in Part #1, but when you scroll down, look for "Micelleanous" and click on "Blog entries"

Include the 10 prompts below so a reader better understands your answers.

  1. What type of food do you most often prepare at home?
  2. What off-campus Terre Haute restaurant do you frequent?
  3. Insert an alive URL link TripAdvisor page that corresponds to the restaurant identified in question #2
  4. List your hobbies
  5. List what other courses are you taking this quarter
  6. Give is your target graduation date.  (For example: Spring 2019)
  7. What degree program(s) are you pursuing?
  8. Have you (or are you currently working) in the computing field?
    If "yes" what type of work do (or did) you do and where?  If "no" what type of computing work do you hope to find?
  9. What other programming languages have you used?
  10. How comfortable to you feel developing software in each of the languages identified in question #9?

Part #3 - Upload a screen shot to this Moodle assignment

  • In Moodle, submit to this assignment a file containing a screen shot of your Moodle blog entry which should include your uploaded picture
  • The screen shot file must be in one of the following formats: png, jpeg, jpg
  • If your blog entry does not fit in one screenful, then shrink it first (ctrl - i.e., minus key)
